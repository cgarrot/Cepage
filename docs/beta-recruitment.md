## Email Template

**Subject:** Beta testers wanted: turn your agent sessions into reusable skills

Hey [Name],

We are about to ship a new feature in Cepage called the **Skill Compiler**, and we would love your help testing it before it goes live.

**What it does.** After you finish a task with Cursor or OpenCode, the Skill Compiler extracts the execution graph, replaces hardcoded values with typed parameters, and turns the whole session into a reusable skill you can run again with different inputs. Think "build once, run many."

**What we need.** We are looking for 10 people to test the full pipeline over one week. You will do two compilations (one from Cursor, one from OpenCode), then fill out a short feedback form. Total time: about 2–3 hours spread across the week.

**What you get.** Early access to a feature that could save you hours per week, direct input on how it works, and a shout-out in the release notes if you want it.

**The catch.** We need honest feedback, not polite encouragement. The single most important question we will ask is: "Would you use this as part of your regular workflow?" We need a clear yes or no.

Interested? Reply to this email and we will send you the setup guide and testing instructions.

Best,
[Your name]
